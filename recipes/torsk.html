Fried Salmon with Potatoes, Melted Butter, Lemons & Cowberries

Here you have my G grandmother Margareta's infamous, one of a kind recipe that I got raised along side. It's truly something else! Do join me down this recipe, as you might just master it with how easy it is!

Ingredients

  • 6 Salmon Fillets
  • 8 Potatoes
  • 4 Lemons
  • 5g Butter
  • However many cowberries you see fit! (alot if you know what's up)
  1. First step is to heat up 3 of your stove plates to medium temperature & have a frying pan, a cooking pot & a smaller cooking pot ready on standby. Tap up some water in the bigger of the pots
  2. Pour up some breadcrumbs on a plate
  3. Now the fun starts! Begin by filleting the salmon into decent sized fillets. Then remove any rogue bones
  4. You then proceed to handle the bone-free salmon in the breadcrumbs you prepared. Really get in there
  5. About time to put in your potatoes in the heated water
  6. Follow that up by frying your fillets
  7. Keep at it until theyre brown-golden & the kitchen smells like heaven
  8. When it's coming to a close you can put the butter in the smaller pot & melt it to a liquid
  9. Happily serve with squeezed lemon juice, cowberries for everybody & the melted butter ontop
