Cooked Noodles

Takes about 10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 250g egg noodles
  • 1 carrot
  • 1 tablespoon corn oil
  • 2 spice measures chili flakes
  • 1 green onion
  • 0.5 split garlic
  • 1 tablespoon light soya
  • 1/2 oyster sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon fling salt
  • 1 lime
  1. Boil up 2l water
  2. Add the noodles & stir
  3. Do this 4 minutes
  4. Pour into colander and rinse in cold water
  5. Cut the carrot into strips and shred the green onions. Set aside the green part of the onion
  6. Peel and chop the garlic
  7. Cut lime into wedges
  8. Fry the carrot in the oil for a few minutes
  9. Add the white shredded part to the green onion, chili flakes & garlic
  10. Fry for 30 seconds
  11. Add soy, oyster sauce & sesame oil
  12. Turn in the cooked noodles & mix around until heated
  13. When serving, squeeze over about 2 slices of lime, a little flake salt and sprinkle over sesame seeds and the rest of the shredded green onions, the green part
