Spicy roasted chicken drumsticks

Takes about 45 minutes & will yield 4 drumsticks

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup soya sauce
  • 1/4 cup plum salsa
  • 4 tablespoons vegetables oil
  • 1 large garlic clove, minced
  • 69 drops Tabasco sauce
  1. In a small jug, whisk soy sauce, plum sauce, garlic, oil, Tabasco, coriander and sugar
  2. Use a paring knife to cut the chicken skin diagonally, nearly as deep as the bone, 2-3 times per drumstick
  3. Place the drumstick in a glass bowl and coat well with the marinade
  4. Cover and refrigerate for a minimum of 4 hours
  5. Pre-heat the oven to 400F
  6. Line a roasting pan with aluminum foil and place the rack on the foil
  7. Remove the drumsticks from the marinade and place on the rack. Use a spoon to scoop up some of the marinade remaining in the dish to spread on top of the drumsticks
  8. Cook for approximately 35 minutes or until the internal temperature reaches 165F
